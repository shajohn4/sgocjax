Who We Are

About Our Parish

St Gregorios Orthodox Church is a vibrant parish rooted in the ancient traditions of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. Named after St Gregorios of Parumala — the first canonized saint from India — our community gathers in worship, fellowship, and service to God and neighbor.

We welcome all who seek a place of prayer, peace, and spiritual nourishment. Our parish is a home for families drawn to the richness of Orthodox Christian faith and the depth of its liturgy, handed down through centuries of devotion.
